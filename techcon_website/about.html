Our History

TechCon began in 2010 as a grassroots effort to bring together innovators in technology. Over the years, it has grown into one of the most respected tech conferences globally.

Our Mission

TechCon exists to inspire innovation, foster collaboration, and advance technology across all sectors. We aim to create an inclusive platform for sharing ideas and building the future.

Notable Past Speakers

Dr. Jane Smith

AI expert and author, Dr. Smith revolutionized conversational AI. Her keynote in 2022 inspired a wave of open-source development.

Mark Johnson

Mark is a pioneer in blockchain and decentralization. He shared insights on building trustless systems at TechCon 2021.

Ayesha Rahman

A leader in tech education, Ayesha emphasized the role of inclusive tech communities in her 2023 session.
